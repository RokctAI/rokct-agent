# Rokct.ai — Vision

## The Brand
Rokct (pronounced "Rocket") is an AI-first platform that runs 
your business, builds your career, manages your life, and 
preserves your legacy — through a single conversation.

## The Name
Rok is the agent. The face of Rokct.
Just as Cursor has Composer, Rokct has Rok.
Rok will evolve from an agent into a model — 
trained on the patterns of human life, work and legacy.

## The Problem
Every tool today knows only a slice of you.
Your accountant knows your numbers.
Your therapist knows your feelings.
Your LinkedIn knows your title.
Nobody knows all three — and how they affect each other.

A bad month in business is often a bad month at home.
A missed target is often a missed night of sleep.
A career plateau is often a personal crisis in disguise.
No tool sees this. Rok does.

## The Solution
Rok is one relationship that spans your entire life:

- Your business — invoices, clients, cash flow, operations
- Your career — milestones, skills, promotions, CV
- Your productivity — goals, weekly wins, accountability
- Your life — memories, achievements, family, health
- Your legacy — obituary evolving in real time, vault,
  executor instructions, end of life wishes

Because Rok holds all of this, it can see what no advisor can:
the intersection of who you are and how you work.

## The Interface
No app to download.
No dashboard to learn.
No form to fill.
Just a conversation — on WhatsApp, the app already open
on every phone in the world.

Rok meets you where you are.

## The Moat
The longer you use Rok, the more it knows you.
The more it knows you, the more irreplaceable it becomes.
10 years of life context cannot be exported to a competitor.
Rok's value compounds with time.

## The Model
Rokct is built on the Frappe stack — headless, modular,
multi-tenant. Any business vertical can be added as a Frappe
app on a tenant. The AI layer is Rok. The interface is
WhatsApp. The memory is the brain.

One backend. One agent. One relationship. Every vertical.

## The Legacy
When a user passes away, Rok already knows their story.
Not from a form they filled in once.
From years of real conversations, real achievements,
real struggles, real wins.

The obituary writes itself.
The vault opens for the right people.
The family is notified.
The business continues or winds down with instructions
already in place.

Rok is the only tool that is still useful after you are gone.
